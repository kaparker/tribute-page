Women in STEM

- Incredible women who changed the world -

Margaret Hamilton

- Computer Scientist -

  • Developed software for predicting weather systems for MIT and lead developer for Apollo flight software at NASA
  • Credited as contibuting to coining the term "software engineering"

Find out more about Margaret Hamilton on Wikipedia.

Katherine Johnson

- Mathematician -

  • Mathematician for NASA initially working as a "computer"
  • Later worked as a aerospace technologist playing a crucial role in calculating trajectories for many NASA missions and verifying the computer's numbers

Find out more about Katherine Johnson on Wikipedia.

Ada Lovelace

- Computer programmer -

  • Widely considered to be the first computer programmer based on her work
  • Realised the potential in machines beyond number crunching

Find out more about Ada Lovelace on Wikipedia.
